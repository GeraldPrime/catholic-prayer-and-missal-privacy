# Privacy Policy for Catholic Prayers And Missal

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Introduction

Catholic Prayers And Missal ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

Catholic Prayers And Missal is designed to work completely offline and does not:
- Collect personal data
- Track user behavior
- Access your contacts, location, or other sensitive information
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Require user accounts or registration
- Connect to external servers (except for optional app updates)

## Data Storage

All content in the app, including:
- Daily Mass readings
- Prayers and devotions
- User preferences

...is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:
- **Internet Access**: Used only for checking for app updates (optional feature)
- **Storage**: Used to cache prayers and readings on your device for offline access

## Third-Party Services

Catholic Prayers And Missal does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.
